Boiled Eggs

Description

Here's how to easily cook boiled eggs.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Water

Steps

  1. Pour cold water into a saucepan.Fill it up until it's a quarter full
  2. Place the desired amount of eggs into the sauce pan. Make sure that they are all lying at the bottom.
  3. Make sure that the eggs are covered by at least an inch or two of water. Add more water if necessary.
  4. Heat the pot/saucepan on high heat and let water boil.
  5. Turn off the heat, keep the pan on the hot burner covered, and let it sit for 10-12 minutes.
  6. Strain the water from the pan and run cold water over the eggs to stop them from cooking further

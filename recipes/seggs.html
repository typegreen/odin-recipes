Scrambled Eggs

Description

Here's how to cook scrambled eggs with a bit of style.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk or Water
  • Olive Oil or Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs in a container
  2. Add milk or water after whisking, then begin beating the eggs again
  3. Gently preheat the pan. Set the heat at low-medium temperatures
  4. Add olive oil or butter on the pan
  5. Start cooking the egg mixture
  6. While cooking, keep moving the mixture inward. Flip when satisfied with texture
  7. When satisfied with appearance, move the eggs to another container and season

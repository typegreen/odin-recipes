Sunny Side Up Eggs

Description

Here's how to cook sunny side up eggs properly.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Olive or Canola Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the nonstick pan/skillet over medium-low heat
  2. Gently crack eggs into the pan. If the eggs make a hissing sound or the whites bubble, turn down the heat.
  3. Cook for 3 minutes or until the whites are mostly set, with some still-runny whites near the yolks.
  4. Tilt the pan forward so that the remaining oil pools. Dip a spoon into the the oil and gently baste the uncooked patches of white until they're set. Don't baste the yolks.
  5. Add preferred seasoning
  6. Remove the eggs from the pan
